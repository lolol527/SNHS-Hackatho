Event Overview

The SNHS STEM Hackathon 2025 is a two-day event where high school students from across Kuwait collaborate in teams to create innovative STEM-based solutions for real-world challenges. The event will be held at the American School of Kuwait from April 23-24 (9 AM - 6 PM daily).

Judging Rubric

  1. Innovation and Creativity (20%)
    • Is the solution novel or unique?
    • Creative alignment with the theme?
    • Original concept or approach?
  2. Impact Potential (30%)
    • Could it have real impact in Kuwait?
    • Does it benefit society, environment, or economy?
    • Addresses critical needs or promotes sustainability?
  3. Technical Implementation (30%)
    • Working prototype or demo?
    • Technically sound and functional?
    • Effective use of tools and tech?
  4. Presentation and Communication (20%)
    • Clear articulation of the problem and solution?
    • Engaging and easy-to-follow presentation?
    • Good Q&A handling and confidence?
