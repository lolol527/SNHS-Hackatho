SNHS Hackathon @ American School of Kuwait

Innovate for Kuwait's Future

Event Schedule

  • Day 1: Opening Ceremony, Team Formation, Problem Reveal, Initial Planning
  • Day 2: Hacking Begins, Mentorship Check-ins, Midpoint Check
  • Day 3: Final Touches, Presentations, Judging, Awards Ceremony

Hackathon Themes & Problem Categories

Theme: Developing Solutions for Issues in Kuwait

  • Environmental
  • Social (Foreigners)
  • Economic
  • Futuristic (Sustainability)
  • Public Transportation
  • Corruption

Project Submission Requirements

  • Presentation: Addressing an ongoing issue, with a feasible and long-term solution
  • Prototype: A tool or machine draft demonstrating functionality
  • Summary: Explanation of how the solution solves the problem, including anticipated challenges and mitigation strategies

Judging Rubric

  1. Innovation and Creativity (20%)
    • Novel solution or unique approach?
    • Creative alignment with the challenge theme?
    • Originality in concept or implementation?
  2. Impact Potential (30%)
    • Impact if implemented?
    • Benefit to Kuwaiti society, environment, or economy?
    • Addresses a critical need or promotes sustainability?
  3. Technical Implementation (30%)
    • Sound and functional technical foundation?
    • Effective use of technology?
    • Demonstrated understanding of the tech?
  4. Presentation and Communication (20%)
    • Clear explanation of the problem and solution?
    • Engaging and concise presentation?
    • Confident answers to questions?
